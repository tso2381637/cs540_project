Introduction

In this project, we present two types of analysis base on the crash data in the U.S.
First type is map: we combined organized crash data with address data, then present the results on the map.
Second type is weather condition: we integrated the crash data with weather by "Date", and statistic the amount of crash happen in each weather condition.

Total amount of data:

15,709,686

Total amount of sources:

48
